How it works

Reporting made effortless

Three simple pillars keep the whole campus community connected and protected.

Easy Reporting

A guided form captures exactly what responders need — incident type, location, time and description — in under a minute.

Fast Response

Every report reaches the admin dashboard instantly, so security teams can triage and act without delay.

Community Safety

Shared visibility into incident status keeps residents, staff and students informed and looking out for one another.
